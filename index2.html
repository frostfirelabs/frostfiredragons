About This Project

Welcome to our Eco-F1 Lab! We are the Frostfire Dragons. We love F1 and also care about a green future. Our project for the Yasin Schools competition shows how fast racing and being eco-friendly can go together.

Fast Cars, Green Planet

We believe that caring for the Earth can make cars even better. This tool lets you explore choices F1 teams make to build a car that's super fast and good for the environment.

How to Use It

This "Eco-F1 Builder" lets you pick different parts for an F1 car. Each part changes:

  • Speed: How fast the car can go.
  • Cost: How much the part costs.
  • Eco-Score: How good it is for the environment (e.g., how much pollution it makes, if it can be recycled).

After you pick your parts, the system calculates your car's total Eco-Score and cost. Then, you can race your car against a "standard" F1 car to see how your eco-friendly choices perform!

Why It Matters

Formula 1 is huge worldwide. By showing new green tech in racing, we can inspire car makers everywhere and teach people about eco-friendly design. Our project shows how racing can help create a better, greener future, one lap at a time.
